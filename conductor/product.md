# Initial Concept
Schoopet is a whimsical AI memory assistant for ADHD, reminders, and habit tracking via SMS.

## Vision
Schoopet is a whimsical AI memory assistant designed to support users with ADHD. Acting as a friendly, magical "sidekick" rather than a sterile tool, it lowers the barrier to personal organization through a familiar and accessible SMS interface.

## Target Audience
- Individuals with ADHD or executive dysfunction.
- Users seeking a low-friction, text-based method for habit tracking and reminders.
- People who prefer a conversational and empathetic interface over rigid productivity apps.

## Key Features
- **Natural Language SMS Interface:** Users interact via simple text messages for reminders, task logging, and general assistance.
- **Conversational Memory:** The AI remembers personal details, context, and past interactions to build a meaningful "relationship" and provide proactive support.
- **Structured Notes & Lists:** Users can manage and query lists (e.g., shopping, books to read) directly through the chat, seamlessly integrating unstructured conversation with structured data.

## Core Pillars
- **Accessibility:** SMS-first design ensures high availability and ease of use.
- **Empathy:** The "sidekick" persona provides encouragement and understanding.
- **Magic:** The experience is designed to feel delightful and helpful, like a magical companion.
