# Product Guidelines

## Communication Style
- **Concise & Direct:** Given the SMS medium, all messages must be brief and to the point.
- **Friendly:** While concise, the tone should remain warm and helpful, avoiding a robotic feel.
- **Supportive:** Responses should always frame interactions positively, especially regarding missed tasks or reminders.

## Design Principles
- **Frictionless Entry:** The primary goal is to capture user intent instantly. Minimize steps to log tasks or notes.
- **Gentle Persistence:** Reminders should be helpful nudges, not nagging notifications. Avoid overwhelming the user.
- **Information Clarity:** Structured data (lists, summaries) must be formatted for maximum readability within the constraints of an SMS bubble.

## User Experience (UX)
- **SMS First:** All core functionality must be fully accessible and optimized for text messaging.
- **Web as Support:** The web interface serves as a landing page and onboarding tool, complementing the primary SMS experience.
